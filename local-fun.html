Local and Regional…

The region around Lake Auger has much to offer. Visitors to the area will find no shortage of things to do and see.

Time to go shopping?

Try one of these pleasant hamlets. Burlington, VT, Plattsburg, NY, Lake Placid, and Keeseville, NY, are all within an hour away.

Local Food and Drink

While dinner at camp is fun, it is always fun to try something new. The Ausable Brewery, is a must for anyone who enjoys a cold beer, time in the country, and good food. Mace Chasm Farm has some staple meats and some specialty products as well. Need some beer, or groceries to go? Check out Arnold's Grocery for an amazing selection of bottled and canned beer.

Adventure

The Adirondacks have plenty in the way of adventure to offer the brave traveller. While the park itself has a lot to offer, there are many other local attractions to check out. Ausable Chasm is close by, and an amazing attraction. Poke-O-Moonshine Mountain is a world class climbing destination drawing people from all over the US and Canada.

If you are coming from or heading to the Vermont side of Lake Champlain, you will probably end up on a ferry. Here you can find info on the Lake Champlain Ferry crossings.
